Razzmatazz


About the Event

   15 Feb'20, 2:00 PM IST - 15 Feb'20, 6:05 PM IST
   Certificates & cash prizes upto Rs. 25,000

Do you dance to express? Do you moonwalk to your bed before you go to sleep? Then what are you waiting for? The perfect platform is here NOW!
Let the Michael Jackson in you dance your worries out.
Free up your mind, loosen the muscles and dance as the beats drop high.
Give a break to your boredom and paint the dance floor with your feet and cool dance moves.

Event Format :

Registration Deadline:
14 Feb'20, 5:00 PM IST

Campus Round: Final Round
15 Feb'20, 2:00 PM IST - 15 Feb'20, 6:00 PM IST

Event Rules :

> There will be two performance categories — Solo/duet and Group
> A group can have a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 15 participants
> They should be from the same institute
> No person can be a part of more than one team
> One team can submit only one entry
> The teams have to register themselves on D2C
> The event is open to all graduation and post-graduation colleges
> There is no restriction on the theme of the performance
> Time Limit:
    Solo/Duet: 2 to 7 mins
    Group: 6 to 12 mins
> Please maintain the performance time limit
> Props, costumes, and others (needed for the performance) must be arranged by the participants
> An instance of vulgarity/disturbing content of any order will lead to the forceful halt of the performance and immediate disqualification
> Teams must report at the location 30 minutes before the competition starts
> The judging criteria will be based on originality, music, stage coverage, co-ordination, dance variation, formations and expressions
> Decision of the judges will be final and abiding
> It is mandatory for the participants to carry valid ID proofs
