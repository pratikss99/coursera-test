Star Night

Melange'20



Every year, after two days of exciting events, Melange hosts the pièce de résistance of the two day festival, the much awaited Star Night, where renowned artists and bands such as Nucleya, Sunburn, Indian Ocean, Tapsee Pannu, Rajkumar Rao have performed in the past for a crowd of over 2000!

This year, Melange '20 hosted three amazing standup comedy artists:
Rahul Subramanian, Rahul Dua and Kumar Varun




Melange '19

DJ Chetas | DJ Seek

Melange '19 hosted a groovy Star Night with the very popular DJ Chetas, the only DJ in Indian History to have cracked into the prestigious DJ MAG awards.




Melange '18


Zakir Khan | Kunal Kamra

Melange '18 witnessed comedians Zakir Khan and Kunal Kamra give a hilarious performance for the audience.
