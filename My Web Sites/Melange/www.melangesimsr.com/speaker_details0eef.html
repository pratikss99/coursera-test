EVENT SPEAKER





Irfan Pathan


Former Indian cricketer Irfan Pathan became a fan favourite for his exploits on the field. The all-rounder from Baroda is the only bowler in the world to take a hat trick in Test cricket in the very first over of the match. The fact that the match was against our arch rival Pakistan and in their home ground Karachi made it all the more memorable.
