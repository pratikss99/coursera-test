EVENT SPEAKER





Ranjit Shetty


Director of CIPL Resurge Private Limited

Ranjit Shetty, a Mechanical Engineer and an MBA in Finance, has over 15 years of experience in the corporate world. He was the Head of the Retail Broking arm of Mafatlal Group.
A short stint in HDFC Bank (of a year) and some hard introspection later he switched careers to join a newly launched Birla Sun Life Insurance in their Marketing Department where he served for the next five years.
In 2006 he joined the ASK Group (erstwhile ASK Raymond James) as the Group Head of Marketing and Communication.
Currently he is the Director of CIPL Resurge Pvt. Ltd.
