EVENT SPEAKER





Dalai Lama


His holiness, The 14th Dalai Lama, the spiritual leader of the Tibetan people is an advocate of compassion and human values to the world. He is the recipient of the 1989 Nobel Peace Prize. He has universal appeal for his kindness and wisdom and is known for his exuberance. The Dalai Lama was on Somaiya Vidyavihar campus to speak on "Living, Loving, Laughing and Dying: The Buddhist Way.
