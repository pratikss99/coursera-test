War of Bands


About the Event

   4 Feb'20, 9:00 AM IST - 14 Feb'20, 9:05 PM IST
   Certificates & Cash prizes upto Rs. 25,000

A battleground where top bands from all around the nation fight it out among themselves for the golden trophy. A celebration of music with powerful riffs and soulful sufis, with the double bass beats and the synchronized headbangs.
You think you have it in you? Well, The Stage Awaits.

Campus Round:

Event Rules :

> A team should consist of 4 to 8 members.
> They should be from the same institute.
> No person can be a part of more than one team.
> One team can submit only one entry.
> There is no restriction on the number of teams from an institute.
> No pre-recorded audio allowed in the event.
> Time limit for each performance: 20 minutes including soundcheck.
