Natsamrat


About the Event

   31 Jan'20, 1:00 PM IST - 13 Feb'20, 5:05 PM IST
   Cash Prize of Rs. 8000/-

The official Dramatics club of SIMSR invites you to be part of "Natsamrat - Theatre and Dramatics Competition" under the festival of MELANGE'20.


Register Now!

Event Format :

> Team size: 1 - 4 members
> Time limit: 5-12 minutes from empty to empty stage.
> This is an On-Campus Round.
> Teams need to send the synopsis of drama/monologue to [email protected]
> Judging Criteria: Teams shall be judged on parameters like creativity, acting, screenplay, time management, and overall impact.
> The teams are free to select the theme/story/script for the skit/drama/monologue.
> Play can be in English, Hindi or bilingual. Teams may use short snippets from other regional languages.

Event Rules :

> The Judge's decision will be final and binding.
> Teams have to bring their own props other than that, If the props are complex and need some assistance from team, then the details should be conveyed beforehand (via mail) at least two days before. Any prop that can harm the stage is strictly prohibited.
> Team Melange will not be responsible for the security of items left behind in the green room or on the stage.
> If a team exceeds the time limit, marks will be deducted.
> The script should be original and not copied.
> No naked flames, spilling of colours dry or wet, or any live animal is allowed.
> Use of vulgar languages is prohibited. Points may be deducted for the same.
> Publicly inappropriate content, blasphemy or content defaming any political party or religious group is not appreciated.
> The participating teams are required that their presentation is keeping with the dignity of the fest.
> Please carry your identity cards
> The organizing team reserves the right to change or modify any of the rules, however, the participants will be informed well in advance.
