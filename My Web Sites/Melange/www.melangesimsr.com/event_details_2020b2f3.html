Abhigrahan


About the Event

   30 Jan'20, 7:30 PM IST - 14 Feb'20, 2:05 PM IST
   Cash prizes upto Rs. 30,000

Are you ready to put your money where your mouth is? Can you strike a balance between Risk & Return?
If yes, then welcome to the big bad world of Mergers & Acquisitions.
We introduce you to Abhigrahan, a single-day event organized by FINSTREET during the flagship event Melange, by KJ SIMSR. This event gives you an opportunity to create a unique portfolio while testing your negotiation, analytical and valuation skills. The ability to gauge the true potential of companies could make you win the event!
What's in it for you?
The opportunity to interact and participate with astute people from B-schools across the country along with a chance to win a cash prize of Rs. 30,000/-


Register Now!

Event Format :

Round 1- Online Round
An online quiz based on fundamentals of finance. Teams will be shortlisted based on the results.
Round 2- Campus Round
Top 8 teams will be taking part in the 2nd round.
This will consist of 2 bidding rounds where the participants will buy and sell companies to make the best portfolio.

Event Rules :

> Participants can register in teams of 2.
> The event will consist of one online & one offline round.
> Based on the performance in the online round participants will move to the offline round.
> The online round will be a quiz round which shall be conducted on 6th February 2020.
> The quiz will contain 25 questions to be answered in 25 minutes.
> The decisions are subject to the discretion of the organizers.
> There are no negative marks in the quiz.
> In case of a tie in the score, the team that finished the quiz earlier will be selected.
> Offline rounds shall be conducted on 14th February 2020 at K.J. SIMSR.
