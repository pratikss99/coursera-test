EVENT SPEAKER





Anupama Chopra


An eminent Indian author, journalist, film critic and director of the MAMI Mumbai Indian Festival, Ms. Anupama Chopra is a woman of multifaceted abilities, having authored National award winning books and hosted a film review show, 'The Front Row with Anupama Chopra'. She is a name to reckon with and an exemplary film critic. We are honoured to have hosted her in Melange'16 as a speaker.
