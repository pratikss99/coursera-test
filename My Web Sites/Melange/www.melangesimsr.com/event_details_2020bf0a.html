About the Event

   15 Feb'20, 10:00 AM IST - 15 Feb'20, 1:00 PM IST
   Cash prizes upto Rs. 8000

On-Campus Management Game

The on-campus round will be a fun decision-making task type of event where teams will be given a list of tasks and will be rewarded points on completion of each task.
The team with the highest point at the end will be declared the winner.
