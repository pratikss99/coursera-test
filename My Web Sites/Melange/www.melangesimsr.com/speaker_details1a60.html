EVENT SPEAKER





Devendra Fadnavis


Former Chief Minister of Maharashtra, Devendra Fadnavis known to be a political leader of the masses and a firm believer in the ideology that Politics is an instrument for socio economic change, Devendra Fadnavis served as the 18th Chief Minister of Maharashtra from 2014 to 2019. He came to our Vidyavihar Campus for inaugurating Maitreyi the new hostel building for girl students of Somaiya Vidyavihar.
